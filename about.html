Our Story

Our History

Attie's Garden started as a backyard ornamental gardening hobby way back in 1989 which blossomed into a family business by a couple, Mr. Noel & Mrs. Fortunata "Attie" Gaurano. And from there, it slowly but steadily found its place in the ornamental industry. This was pioneered by the budding couple who have the green thumbs and passion for plants.
